How Intelligence Is Built
Every Climactix score is derived from a structured chain: raw enterprise data is validated against evidence, normalized to sector-specific benchmarks, weighted by financial materiality, and scored across eight intelligence dimensions. The output is a letter-rated climate intelligence profile — interpretable by risk officers, portfolio managers, and regulators.
Evidence Validation — Every score input is validated against uploaded documents, third-party data, and cross-referenced against disclosure records
Sector-Specific Models — Each of 26 industries has its own materiality mapping, indicator weights, and benchmark thresholds
Confidence Scoring — Every intelligence output includes a confidence level derived from data completeness and evidence quality
Explainable Scoring — No black-box outputs. Every rating contains reasoning layers accessible to analysts, auditors, and regulators
Framework Alignment — Outputs are mapped to TCFD, BRSR, CSRD, ISSB, GRI, and CDP frameworks simultaneously
