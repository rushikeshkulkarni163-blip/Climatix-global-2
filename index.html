Vision

To establish a structured and verifiable framework for ESG communication, enabling transparency, consistency, and data-driven stakeholder engagement.

Climactix Global aims to establish first data-driven green media and ESG communications ecosystem. By integrating AI-powered ESG narrative engines, blockchain-based transparency, and multi-format media channels, we transform sustainability metrics — aligned with GRI, SASB, TCFD, and CSRD — into verifiable, compelling, investor-ready narratives.

The vision: enable corporations, startups, governments, and investors to amplify sustainability impact through transparent communication that builds trust, compliance, and stakeholder engagement. Climactix Global will be the platform where ESG assets are supported by automated, verifiable storytelling.

Problem Statement

Why ESG Communication is Broken

Despite $30T+ in ESG investment, companies face critical gaps in translating performance into credible, accessible narratives.

📊
ESG Communication Gap
Disclosures are data-heavy and compliance-oriented — inaccessible to investors, employees, and the public.
🚨
Greenwashing Risk
67% of private investors doubt ESG claims. Stakeholders distrust sustainability assertions without audit trails.
📰
Media Fragmentation
Climate narratives are scattered across platforms — making verification and comparison near impossible.
⚖️
Regulatory Pressure
CSRD, SEC Climate Rules, and ISSB create growing compliance challenges while demanding investor-friendly narratives.
🗄️
Data Silos
ESG data sits disconnected across finance, operations, CSR, and supply chain — preventing holistic storytelling.
💰
Cost & Efficiency Gap
Traditional ESG reporting costs $197K–$739K annually per company — crushing SMEs and startups.
📉
Investor Disconnect
83% of investors use ESG data yet 62% cite lack of clarity and 63% cite inconsistencies. They need real-time insights.
🌏
India Market Gap
India — 3rd largest emitter — lacks dedicated climate dialogue platforms engaging policymakers, entrepreneurs, and youth.

MVP Product Roadmap

Three Phases to Full ESG Operating System

Phase 1 · 0–6 months
Foundation

Build the core AI narrative engine and analytics dashboards.

  • ESG data ingestion (PDF, DOCX, XLSX, API)
  • GRI, SASB, TCFD data normalization
  • AI narrative generator (investor briefs, social, reports)
  • SDG semantic tagging via NLP
  • Narrative Reach Index, Trust Index, SDG Score
  • Social auto-post + PDF export
Phase 2 · 6–12 months
Verification & Engagement

Build trust + community engagement ecosystem.

  • Blockchain verification (Polygon)
  • Immutable ESG audit trails
  • Smart contracts for NGO/auditor validation
  • Community campaign module (carbon credits)
  • AI podcast scripts + video storyboards
  • Sentiment analysis + competitor benchmarking
Phase 3 · 12–24 months
IoT + Real-Time ESG OS

Full real-time data-to-storytelling operating system.

  • CO₂ sensors → live emissions dashboard
  • Energy + water + soil sensor integration (MQTT)
  • AI reputation + ESG risk prediction engine
  • Crisis narrative suggestions
  • Investor Dashboard Pro with SDG/region filters
  • Real-time ESG impact streams

The Founder

Rushikesh Kulkarni

Rushikesh Kulkarni is the Founder and Director of Climactix Global, an emerging platform focused on improving how climate and ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) initiatives are communicated and understood.

With a background in sustainability and operations, Rushikesh is working to address a key challenge in the climate space—the gap between complex ESG data and clear, accessible communication. Through Climactix Global, he aims to develop structured, credible, and human-centric narratives that help organizations, governments, and institutions better articulate their sustainability efforts.

His broader vision is to build a reliable communication layer for climate and ESG initiatives, enabling greater transparency, stakeholder engagement, and informed decision-making.

As part of this effort, he has initiated Sunday Climate Talks, a podcast series designed to bring together diverse voices—including policymakers, entrepreneurs, and practitioners—to discuss practical climate solutions in a simplified and relatable manner.

Rushikesh’s work is driven by a focus on bridging knowledge, communication, and on-ground impact, with an emphasis on making sustainability more understandable and actionable across different audiences.
